03 — Research

Active Research
Projects.

In Progress
EDMDc-Based Predictive Control for Real-Time Drone Interception

Applying Extended Dynamic Mode Decomposition with control (EDMDc) to learn a linear Koopman operator representation of nonlinear drone dynamics. The learned model is used within a Model Predictive Control framework to enable real-time interception of autonomous aerial targets with minimal computational overhead.

In Progress
Desensitized i-PID for Precise Motion Control in UAVs

Extending the intelligent PID (i-PID) framework with a desensitization approach to reduce sensitivity to parameter uncertainty and external disturbances. The work targets high-precision motion control for UAV platforms where classical PID tuning is insufficient under varying payload and environmental conditions.
